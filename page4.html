RAVINDRA JADEJA

Ravindrasinh Anirudhsinh Jadeja (born 6 December 1988) is an Indian international cricketer who represents the Indian national cricket team in ODI and Test formats.
He is an all-rounder, who bats left-handed and bowls left-arm orthodox spin.

A Jadeja dynasty ruled the princely state of Kutch between 1540 and 1948, at which time India became a republic.
This state had been formed by king Khengarji.
